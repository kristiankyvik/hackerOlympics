Almirall
Uniquely,
exclusive

Located on top of the mountain of Tibidabo, overlooking beautiful Barcelona, the Old Library of Valentí Almirall is a dream spot for singular events.

The history of this emblematic building from the 20th century is tied to the life of Valentí Almirall Llozer, one of Catalonia's most important intellectual characters of the second half of the 19th century, both from a cultural and political perspective. It was built after his death as a result of his widow's desire to have a private library next to their house.

The lecture room of the library is the heart of the building.
It is a tall and luminous space with a central skylight that provides natural sunlight in the daytime and a stunning view of the stars at nighttime. A perfect location for dinners, coctels or even parties.
